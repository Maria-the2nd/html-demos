FAQ

Questions you're probably asking

Do I need to code?

No. You can if you want to, but Flow Bridge handles the translation. If you can copy and paste, you're qualified.

Does this replace Webflow?

God no. This feeds Webflow. Webflow is the editing superpower‚Äîwe just get your AI-generated stuff into it without the pain.

What AI tools do you support?

Anything that outputs HTML/CSS/JS. Cursor, v0, Bolt, Claude Artifacts, ChatGPT, that random tool you found on Product Hunt at 2am. If it makes web code, we'll convert it.

Will it be perfect every time?

No. It'll be editable every time. That's the point. AI outputs are messy. Flow Bridge makes them workable. You make them great.

What about custom code and animations?

We preserve JavaScript where possible and flag complex interactions that might need manual attention. The goal is "mostly works immediately, fully works with light touch-ups."
