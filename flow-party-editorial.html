FAQ

Common questions about Flow Party.

How does copy & paste work? 01

Click copy on any component in The Stash, then paste directly into your Webflow Designer. The component arrives with all classes, interactions, and settings intact.

What's included in a Party Pack? 02

Each Party Pack includes a complete Webflow template, matching component library, Figma source files, and a detailed launch guide.

Can I use resources for client projects? 03

Yes! All resources come with a commercial license. Use them for unlimited personal and client projects.

Do I need GSAP knowledge? 04

Nope. Interactions work out of the box. But if you want to customize, we include documentation explaining how each animation works.
